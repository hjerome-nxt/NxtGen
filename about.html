About Us

Driven by Purpose,
Powered by Innovation

NxtGen Technologies LLC is a San Diego-based technology company building intelligent platforms that address the world's most pressing wellness and safety challenges.

Our Mission

To harness the power of artificial intelligence and thoughtful design to create platforms that make mental wellness, safety, and education accessible to everyone — regardless of language, location, or economic status.

Our Vision

A world where every individual has access to the tools and support they need for mental wellness — where technology bridges gaps in care, empowers educators, and strengthens communities.

Our Journey

From Vision to Global Impact

Early Career

Technology & Safety Innovation

Our founder Hubert Jerome began his career in wearable technology and safety innovation, holding leadership positions at Tile/Life360 — pioneering family safety and location-sharing technology.

2020

Trase: COVID Response

During the pandemic, designed Trase — an automated contact tracing platform deployed across 150+ community college campuses, serving over 2.1 million students and keeping communities safer.

2022

NxtGen Technologies Founded

Established NxtGen Technologies LLC in San Diego, CA — with a mission to build AI-powered solutions for mental wellness, safety, and education at a global scale.

2023

My Safe Spaces Launches

Launched My Safe Spaces — India's first integrated mental wellness ecosystem — with programs spanning schools (SWISS), workplaces (CREW), personal wellness, and professional development (ELEVATE).

2024-Present

Scaling Impact

IntellicareAI launches as a multilingual AI wellness companion. Strategic partnerships with India Mental Health Alliance, Mindful Dignity Lab, and TAISI expand our reach to 1M+ students.

Leadership

Meet Our Founder

Hubert Jerome

Founder & CEO

A visionary technologist and wellness advocate, Hubert Jerome brings deep expertise in wearable technology, safety innovation, and AI-driven platforms. From pioneering family safety technology at Tile/Life360 to designing COVID-era contact tracing solutions, Hubert has consistently built technology that protects and empowers communities.

Today, he leads NxtGen Technologies and its flagship platforms — My Safe Spaces and My Safe Schools — with a focus on making mental wellness accessible to millions across India and beyond.

Our Values

What Guides Everything We Do

01

Evidence-Based

Every product we build is grounded in rigorous research — from randomized controlled trials to peer-reviewed meta-analyses.

02

Accessibility First

We design for everyone — supporting 6+ languages, mobile-first experiences, and pricing models that don't exclude those who need help most.

03

Human-Centered AI

Our AI augments human care — it never replaces it. Crisis detection always escalates to real people, and technology serves the human, not the other way around.

04

Measurable Impact

We obsess over outcomes, not outputs. From effect sizes of 0.72+ to 4.2x ROI, we prove that doing good and doing well aren't mutually exclusive.
